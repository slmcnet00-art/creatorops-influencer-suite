CreatorOps Influencer Suite Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

CreatorOps Influencer Suite is an influencer campaign management platform operated by Mipingplan.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information such as name, email address, team, and role.
  • Campaign information entered by users, including brand name, product details, campaign brief, target persona, keywords, schedules, and KPI targets.
  • Public creator and content metadata from third-party platforms, including public channel URLs, public video URLs, subscriber or follower counts where available, public view counts, public like counts, public comment counts, publish dates, and thumbnails.
  • Outreach and workflow records, including candidate status, message status, notes, and report data entered or generated by users.
  • Technical information such as browser type, device information, usage logs, and error logs for security and service improvement.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide creator discovery, campaign management, outreach preparation, and reporting features.
  • Help users evaluate public creator fit for brand campaigns.
  • Generate campaign strategy, outreach messages, content guides, and reports using AI-assisted workflows.
  • Track public campaign content performance after users register content URLs.
  • Maintain security, prevent abuse, debug issues, and improve service reliability.

3. YouTube API Services

CreatorOps Influencer Suite uses the YouTube Data API to access publicly available YouTube channel and video data. We do not use the YouTube Data API to access private YouTube user data.

Public YouTube data may be used for creator discovery, content reference search, and campaign performance reporting. By using features that rely on YouTube data, users also agree to the YouTube Terms of Service and the Google Privacy Policy.

4. Data Sharing and Retention

We do not sell personal information. We may share data with service providers only as necessary to operate the platform, including hosting, database, email delivery, analytics, AI processing, and API integration providers.

We retain account, campaign, and workflow data as long as needed to provide the service or comply with legal and operational requirements. Users may request deletion of their account or campaign data by contacting us.

5. User Control and Deletion

Users may request access, correction, export, or deletion of their account and campaign data. Users may also revoke connected Google account access from their Google Account permissions page when OAuth features are used.

6. Security and Contact

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect information. However, no internet-based service can guarantee absolute security.

For privacy questions or deletion requests, contact mipingplanai@gmail.com.
